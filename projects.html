Projects

Detailed case studies of my production work, from concept to final mix.

2024 · Album Production

"Echoes" - Full Album Production

Complete album production for emerging artist Sarah Chen. 12 tracks blending electronic and organic elements, creating a cohesive sonic journey that explores themes of memory and identity.

  • • 12 original compositions
  • • Full mixing and mastering
  • • Sound design and synthesis
  • • Collaborative creative direction

The Process

The project began with extensive pre-production meetings to understand Sarah's vision. We established a sonic palette that would serve as the foundation for the entire album—warm analog synths, organic percussion, and ethereal vocal processing.

Each track was built from the ground up, with careful attention to how they would flow together as a complete listening experience. The mixing process involved multiple revisions to ensure each element had its place in the stereo field, while mastering brought cohesion and commercial loudness without sacrificing dynamics.

2024 · Film Score

"The Last Light" - Film Score

Original score composition for independent film "The Last Light." Orchestral arrangements with electronic textures, enhancing the narrative's emotional depth and supporting the film's visual storytelling.

  • • 45 minutes of original music
  • • Hybrid orchestral/electronic approach
  • • Real-time synchronization with picture
  • • Dynamic range optimization for cinema

The Challenge

The director wanted a score that felt both timeless and contemporary—classical orchestral elements that could seamlessly blend with modern electronic production. This required careful orchestration and sound design to create a cohesive hybrid sound.

Working closely with the director and editor, I developed themes for each character and key emotional moments. The score needed to support the narrative without overwhelming it, leaving space for dialogue and ambient sound design. The final mix was optimized for both theatrical and streaming distribution.

2023 · EP Production

"Midnight Sessions" - EP

Production and mixing for a 5-track EP exploring nocturnal soundscapes. Dark, atmospheric hip-hop with electronic elements, featuring collaborations with multiple vocalists.

  • • 5-track cohesive EP
  • • Multiple artist collaborations
  • • Atmospheric sound design
  • • Streaming-optimized mastering

The Vision

The concept was to create a cohesive listening experience that captured the energy and atmosphere of late-night creative sessions. Each track was designed to flow into the next, creating a continuous journey through different moods and tempos.

The production process involved extensive sound design sessions to create unique textures and atmospheres. Each vocalist brought their own style, and my role was to create a sonic foundation that would complement and enhance their performances while maintaining the overall EP's cohesive sound.
